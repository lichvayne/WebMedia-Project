ABOUT US

WebMedia Inc offers a unique service to all investors and home buyers interested in benefiting from the real estate opportunities in Georgia’s capital, guiding potential buyers through the entire purchasing process from start to finish

PURCHASING

First we listen to your requirements and then we show you the best offers that meet with your budget and purchasing goals. We only work with the biggest, most reliable and experienced developers to ensure that your investment is secure

REGISTRATION

We help you to register the property at the Public Registry Office. If you require Permanent Residence in Georgia, we can assist you with the process

FINISHING

Most apartments in Tbilisi are sold on a White Frame basis. Our Construction Division will work with buyers to finish their apartments to their specifications and budget – usually within six weeks

INTERIOR DESIGN

Our Interior Design Division can then take over and bring your apartment up to full ready-to-rent status, right down to soft-furnishings and cutlery.

RENTAL MANAGEMENT

Once the apartment is up to full turnkey standard, our Rental Management Division will market your property in order to achieve the highest yields for investors. Professional property photographers will ensure that your apartment really stands out from the crowd. We deposit your rental receipts into a bank account of your choosing on a regular basis.

All our services are completely free of charge to buyers – with the exception of Rental Management services, where we take a small commission from all rental income earned.

Tbilisi Property is the only agency in Georgia to offer such a comprehensive service to foreign real estate buyers. With over a decade’s experience in the international real estate business, we are experts in the field and offer British levels of service to all our clients.
